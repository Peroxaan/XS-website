Privacy Policy

Here at Xenoshare, we care about your privacy and security on this platform.

In Xenoshare, all login data is handled using third party login services, such as Sign in with Apple, Sign in with Google, etc. We don’t keep any login information on you, so you don’t have to worry about your passwords being potentially breached with us.

On top of that, we don’t collect any user information on their usage of our apps and platform. In fact, we only store data that you provide - and nothing else. The only data that we store relates to your profile information, and your post information.

Xenoshare is early in development and things will change as our platform progresses, but for now - this is our stance on privacy.
